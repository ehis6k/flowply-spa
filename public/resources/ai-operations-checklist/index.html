AI Operations Checklist

A practical checklist for assessing your AI automation's production readiness. Covers monitoring, escalation, change management, and compliance.

Checklist

  • Monitoring: Define SLOs/SLAs, instrument key metrics, and configure alerting with escalation policies.
  • Observability: Centralized logs, traces, and retention policy for audit and root-cause analysis.
  • Escalation: Clear on-call rotations, human-in-the-loop processes, and documented runbooks.
  • Change Management: CI/CD gating, canary/gradual rollout, and rollback plans.
  • Data & Privacy: Data lineage, masking, and access controls for sensitive inputs/outputs.
  • Testing & Validation: Pre-prod testing, drift detection, and post-deploy health checks.
  • Compliance & Audit: Logging of decisions, versioning of models, and retention of records.
